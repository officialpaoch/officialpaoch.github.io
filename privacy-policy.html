Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

Paoch ("the App") is a cosmetic tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Paoch, you may enter product information such as names, brands, categories, open dates, and personal ratings. You may also capture or select product photos using your device's camera or photo library. All of this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server operated by us.

Information Collected Automatically

Paoch does not collect analytics, device identifiers, usage statistics, or any other information automatically.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Paoch, including products, routines, preferences, and images, is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. We do not operate servers that receive or store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

Paoch interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Open Beauty Facts (world.openbeautyfacts.org): When you scan a product barcode, the App sends the barcode number to the Open Beauty Facts public API to retrieve product information (name, brand, category, and image). No personal information is included in this request. Open Beauty Facts' privacy policy governs their handling of API requests.
  • Amazon product search links: When you choose to open a product link, the App opens an Amazon search results page in your external browser using the product name and brand as search terms. Amazon's own privacy policy governs information processed after its website opens.

Device Permissions

Paoch requests the following device permissions, each of which you may grant or deny:

  • Camera: Used to scan barcodes and take product photos.
  • Photo Library: Used to select existing images for products.
  • Notifications: Used to send local reminders about product expiration dates. These notifications are generated entirely on your device; no push notification service is involved.

Data Export and Backup

Paoch allows you to export your data as a backup file. This file is generated and stored locally on your device. You control where this file goes; it is not uploaded to any service by the App.

Children's Privacy

Paoch does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because the App stores all data locally and does not transmit personal information, there is no mechanism through which we could collect or access a child's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at officialpaoch@gmail.com.
