Your beauty collection,
organized and on track.

Paoch helps you track expiration dates, build skincare routines, and manage your cosmetic collection. Everything stays private, on your device.

Features

📅

Expiration Tracking

Never wonder if a product is still good. Track opening dates and get notified before items expire based on their PAO (period after opening).

📷

Barcode Scanner

Scan a product barcode to instantly look up its name, brand, and category. Adding products takes seconds, not minutes.

📚

Product Collection

Organize your entire beauty collection in one place: skincare, makeup, hair, fragrance, and more. Filter by category, status, or brand.

⛰

Routines

Build morning and evening skincare routines. Assign products to each step and follow your routine day by day.

📈

Insights

See trends across your collection, like top categories, products nearing expiration, and repurchase history.

🔒

Private by Design

All your data stays on your device. No account required, no cloud sync, no analytics. Export a backup anytime you want.

Who Is Paoch For?

Anyone who owns more beauty products than they can keep track of. Whether you're managing a skincare routine, trying to use products before they expire, or simply want to know what you own, Paoch keeps it all organized without the spreadsheet hassle.
